Where Happy Scribe Wins

  • Human transcription option: Happy Scribe offers professional human transcription at EUR 1.70/minute for situations where 100% accuracy is essential (legal, medical, broadcast). Bolo Aur Likho is AI-only.
  • Subtitle editor: Happy Scribe has a polished subtitle editor with SRT/VTT export, timing adjustment, and a built-in video player. This is superior for video creators who need subtitles.
  • Export variety: Happy Scribe exports to DOCX, PDF, SRT, VTT, and more. Bolo Aur Likho currently offers plain text export only.
  • European language accuracy: Being a European company, Happy Scribe has strong accuracy for French, German, Spanish, Portuguese, Dutch, and other European languages.
  • Developer API: Happy Scribe offers a well-documented API for programmatic transcription integration.

Where Bolo Aur Likho Wins

  • Hindi and Indian languages: Bolo Aur Likho outputs Devanagari script, handles Hinglish, and supports 10 Indian languages with dedicated optimization. Happy Scribe lists Hindi but is not optimized for Indian languages.
  • Completely free: No per-minute pricing, no credit card. Happy Scribe charges EUR 0.20/minute even for AI transcription a 30-minute file costs EUR 6.
  • No signup: Upload and transcribe instantly. Happy Scribe requires account creation.
  • AI summary: Bolo Aur Likho generates an AI summary with key points. Happy Scribe provides transcription only.
  • Privacy: Audio is deleted immediately after processing. Happy Scribe stores files on their servers.
