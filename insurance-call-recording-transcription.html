Why Insurance Companies Need Call Transcription

IRDAI Compliance

IRDAI mandates that insurance companies record and retain tele-sales conversations. Transcription turns these recordings into searchable text that compliance teams can audit efficiently. Instead of listening to random samples, teams can search all calls for specific phrases: "guaranteed returns," "no exclusions," or "full coverage" — the language patterns that indicate potential mis-selling.

Mis-selling Detection

Insurance mis-selling is a major regulatory concern in India. AI transcription combined with keyword monitoring can flag calls where agents may have made unauthorized promises, failed to disclose exclusions, or pressured customers. This shifts quality auditing from reactive (waiting for complaints) to proactive (catching issues before they escalate).

Claims Dispute Resolution

When customers dispute coverage or claim what was promised during the sales call, having a text transcript provides clear evidence. Transcripts are far more practical than asking arbitrators or ombudsmen to listen to audio recordings.

Agent Training and Performance

Transcripts of top-performing agents become training material for new hires. Managers can identify which phrases, objection-handling techniques, and disclosure approaches correlate with successful sales and lower complaint rates.
