Frequently Asked Questions

How do I export an iPhone voice memo as M4A?
Open the Voice Memos app, tap the recording, tap the three dots (…), and select Share. You can AirDrop it to your computer or email it to yourself.
Can I transcribe WhatsApp voice notes in M4A format?
Yes. Save the WhatsApp audio note to your device and upload it directly to Bolo Aur Likho.
Is M4A the same as AAC?
M4A is a file container format that uses AAC audio compression. Both refer to the same type of audio content.
