Why Indian Teams Are Looking for a Gong Alternative

Gong.io is one of the best conversation intelligence platforms in the world — for large US enterprise sales teams with healthy USD budgets. For a 20-person tele-sales team in Pune or a 50-agent BPO in Gurugram, the economics simply do not work.

Here is what Indian sales managers tell us when they switch:

  • Pricing does not fit Indian budgets. Gong charges $100 to $200 per seat per month, billed annually, in USD. For a 30-agent team, that is Rs 25 to 50 lakh per year — before implementation.
  • Hindi and Hinglish are not properly supported. Gong is optimised for English. Indian tele-sales calls, which mix Hindi, regional languages, and English mid-sentence, produce poor transcripts and unreliable sentiment scores.
  • Minimum seat requirements. Gong requires a minimum of 5 to 10 seats just to get started, making it unsuitable for small or growing teams.
  • Long implementation cycles. Gong implementations typically take weeks with CRM integration, admin setup, and training. Most Indian SMB teams cannot afford that runway.
💡 Gong's list price starts at approximately $100 per user per month, billed annually. For a 25-seat team, that is Rs 20 to 25 lakh per year at current exchange rates.
