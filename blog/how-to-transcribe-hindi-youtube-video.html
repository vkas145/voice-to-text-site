How to Transcribe Hindi YouTube Videos for Free

Vikas Omprakash Goyal Apr 13, 2026 8 min read

Hindi is the third most-used language on YouTube globally. From educational channels and tech reviews to comedy sketches and news commentary, Hindi YouTube content reaches hundreds of millions of viewers. But YouTube's auto-generated Hindi captions are often unreliable full of errors, missing Devanagari text, and poor with Hinglish content. Here's how to get accurate Hindi transcripts from any YouTube video, completely free.

Why Transcribe Hindi YouTube Videos?

  • Students studying from Hindi channels take better notes from educational content by UPSC prep channels, coding tutorials in Hindi, or science explainers
  • Content repurposing turn your Hindi YouTube video into a blog post, social media captions, or an article
  • SEO for YouTube creators add accurate Hindi transcripts as descriptions or closed captions to boost discoverability
  • Journalists and researchers transcribe Hindi interviews, speeches, or news segments for reference
  • Accessibility provide accurate captions for deaf and hard-of-hearing viewers
  • Translation preparation having Hindi text makes it easier to translate content to English or other languages

Step-by-Step: Transcribe Hindi YouTube Video

Step 1: Get the audio from the YouTube video

You need the audio file from the YouTube video. There are several ways to do this:

  • Browser-based tools: Use any free online YouTube to MP3 converter. Search for "YouTube to MP3" and paste the video URL. Download the audio as MP3.
  • yt-dlp (command line): For tech-savvy users, yt-dlp -x --audio-format mp3 VIDEO_URL extracts the audio directly.
  • If it's your own video: Download the original audio file from YouTube Studio.

Step 2: Upload to Bolo Aur Likho

Go to boloaurlikho.com and upload the audio file. Supports MP3, WAV, M4A, OGG, and more. No signup required.

Step 3: Select Hindi and transcribe

Choose "Hindi" from the language dropdown. This tells Whisper to optimize for Hindi speech patterns and Devanagari output. Click "Transcribe Now".

Step 4: Review and use your transcript

Within seconds, you'll have a complete Hindi transcript in Devanagari script. If you enabled timestamps, each section will be marked with [MM:SS] markers for easy reference. Copy, download, or use the text however you need.

YouTube's auto-generated captions for Hindi are often problematic. Bolo Aur Likho, powered by OpenAI Whisper, provides significantly better accuracy for Hindi content because:

Use Cases by Content Type

Educational Content (UPSC, Engineering, Medical)

Hindi-medium educational channels are extremely popular. Transcribing these lectures gives students searchable, printable study material. You can highlight key concepts, create flashcards, and review without rewatching hours of video.

News and Commentary

Hindi news channels and political commentary often include rapid speech and multiple speakers. Bolo Aur Likho handles these well, providing a text record that's easier to reference than scrubbing through video.

Entertainment and Interviews

Hindi interviews with actors, musicians, or public figures can be transcribed for articles, fan content, or research. The Hinglish support is particularly valuable here since most Hindi interviews naturally mix languages.
