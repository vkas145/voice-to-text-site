Other TurboScribe Alternatives Worth Knowing

Riverside.fm

Riverside offers unlimited free transcription with no signup, supporting 100+ languages. It's strong if you're in the podcast/creator space. However, it's primarily a recording platform, the standalone transcription tool is simpler and less feature-rich than Bolo Aur Likho.

Otter.ai

Otter.ai is excellent for live meeting transcription, with direct integrations for Zoom, Google Meet, and Teams. The free plan gives you 300 minutes per month. If you need meeting-specific features like speaker labels and live captions, Otter.ai is worth considering, but for file upload transcription with no monthly cap, Bolo Aur Likho wins.

NoteGPT

NoteGPT offers free transcription with no signup and supports large files. It's a solid option, but the interface is more cluttered and the AI summary quality is inconsistent compared to Bolo Aur Likho's GPT-powered summaries.
