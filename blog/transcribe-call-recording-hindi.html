Why Transcribe Hindi Call Recordings?

Sales Teams

Indian sales teams conduct most calls in Hindi or Hinglish. Transcribing sales calls helps managers review pitch quality, train new reps, track commitments made to customers, and identify winning patterns. Instead of listening to hours of recordings, managers can skim transcripts in minutes.

Customer Service

Customer support teams handle complaint calls, refund requests, and service issues in Hindi. Transcripts create an auditable trail, help resolve disputes, and provide training material for new agents. They also feed into quality audits.

Legal and Compliance

Lawyers, consultants, and compliance teams often record calls for documentation purposes. Hindi transcripts of these calls serve as evidence, reference material, and compliance records. Having text is far more practical than storing audio files.

Personal Records

Important personal calls with doctors, insurance agents, real estate brokers, or government offices often contain crucial details. A transcript means you never lose important information discussed over the phone.

How to Record Phone Calls

On Android

Most Android phones (especially Samsung, Xiaomi, Realme, OnePlus) have built-in call recording. Go to Phone app settings and enable "Auto record calls" or tap the record button during a call. Recordings are saved as MP3 or M4A files in your phone's storage.

On iPhone

iPhone doesn't have native call recording. You can use the speakerphone method put the call on speaker and record using another device or a voice recorder app on a second phone. Some third-party apps also offer call recording via three-way calling.

Always inform the other party that the call is being recorded. Recording consent laws vary by state in India, but transparency is always the best practice.
