The Key Difference: Meeting Calls vs Phone Calls

MeetRecord was primarily designed for inside sales teams that use video conferencing — Zoom, Google Meet, Teams. It excels at recording and analysing B2B discovery and demo calls conducted over video.

Bolo Aur Likho works best for teams with phone-based calls — insurance agents, tele-sales, BFSI advisors, BPO contact centres — where calls are recorded as audio files (MP3, WAV, M4A) rather than via a meeting bot. If your team's calls happen over the phone rather than video conferencing, Bolo Aur Likho's workflow fits more naturally.
