Where Otter.ai Wins

Otter.ai is genuinely better than Bolo Aur Likho in several areas:

  • Live meeting integration: Otter.ai can join Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams meetings automatically and transcribe in real-time. Bolo Aur Likho requires you to record and upload separately.
  • Speaker identification: Otter.ai automatically labels who said what in multi-speaker conversations. This is valuable for meeting minutes and interview transcription.
  • Team collaboration: Shared workspaces, commenting, and highlighting make Otter.ai better for teams who review transcripts together.
  • Mobile app: Otter.ai has dedicated iOS and Android apps for on-the-go recording and transcription.
  • English accuracy: For native English speakers in quiet environments, Otter.ai's proprietary model is competitive with Whisper on English-only content.

Where Bolo Aur Likho Wins

  • Hindi and Indian languages: This is the biggest differentiator. Bolo Aur Likho outputs Hindi in Devanagari script, handles Hinglish code-switching, and supports Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Marathi, Urdu, Punjabi, Gujarati, Kannada, and Malayalam. Otter.ai does not provide native Indic script output.
  • Truly free: No 300-minute monthly cap, no signup, no credit card. Otter.ai's free tier is restrictive enough to push most users toward the $16.99/month plan.
  • Privacy: Your audio is processed and immediately deleted. Otter.ai stores recordings on their servers, which may be a concern for sensitive content like legal or medical recordings.
  • No account required: Upload and transcribe instantly without creating an account. Useful for one-time transcriptions or users who don't want another SaaS subscription.
  • 99+ languages: Powered by Whisper's multilingual model, Bolo Aur Likho supports far more languages than Otter.ai.
