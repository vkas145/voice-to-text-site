The Real Reason Indian Sales Teams Switch

Jiminny is built for an English-language, SaaS B2B sales workflow — typically an SDR or AE team closing deals over video calls, with CRM hygiene baked in. Most Indian sales contact centres operate differently: high-volume outbound phone calls, Hindi and regional language interactions, and QA processes focused on compliance and script adherence rather than deal momentum.

The language gap is decisive. A coaching platform that cannot accurately transcribe Hindi will generate garbage coaching insights — even if the platform itself is well-designed. Bolo Aur Likho processes Indian language calls accurately first, then makes that transcript available for coaching, compliance review, and scoring.

For teams doing 200 to 2,000 calls per day in Hindi and Hinglish, the workflow is: upload the day's calls in batch, review AI transcripts for quality and compliance flags, run coaching sessions anchored on specific transcript quotes. No CRM integration required for that workflow to deliver coaching value.

💡 If your sales team uses a mix of Hindi and English in the same call — code-switching, which is standard in Indian B2B and B2C sales — Bolo Aur Likho handles it natively. Most English-trained transcription tools produce garbled output for Hinglish. Accurate transcription is the foundation of accurate coaching.
