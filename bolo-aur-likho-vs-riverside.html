Where Riverside Wins

  • Podcast and video recording: Riverside is a full recording studio in the browser. It records separate high-quality tracks for each participant, which is essential for professional podcast production. Bolo Aur Likho does not record remote guests.
  • Built-in video editor: Riverside's text-based editor lets you edit video by editing the transcript. Delete a sentence from the transcript, and the corresponding video is cut automatically.
  • Magic Clips: Riverside uses AI to identify the most engaging moments and generates short clips for social media automatically.
  • Multi-track recording: Each participant's audio and video is recorded separately, so if one person has a bad connection, only their track is affected.
  • Screen recording and live streaming: Riverside supports screen sharing and can stream directly to YouTube/LinkedIn.

Where Bolo Aur Likho Wins

  • Transcription-first: Bolo Aur Likho is built for one thing — accurate audio transcription. If you already have a recording and just need text, it is faster and simpler than Riverside.
  • Hindi and Indian languages: Devanagari output, Hinglish support, and 10 dedicated Indian language pages. Riverside is primarily used by English-language podcasters.
  • Completely free: No $15/month subscription. No per-minute pricing. No signup. Riverside's free tier is very limited (2 hours of recording).
  • Any audio file: Upload MP3, WAV, M4A, OGG, FLAC from any source. Riverside's transcription is primarily designed for recordings made within Riverside.
  • Privacy: Audio is processed and immediately deleted. Riverside stores recordings in their cloud.

Who Should Use Which?

Choose Riverside if:

  • You are a podcaster who needs to record remote guests with studio quality
  • You need video recording + editing + transcription in one platform
  • You want AI-generated social media clips from your episodes
  • You primarily produce English-language content

Choose Bolo Aur Likho if:

  • You already have audio recordings and just need accurate transcription
  • You need Hindi, Urdu, or other Indian language transcription
  • You want a free tool with no subscription commitment
  • Privacy matters — you don't want recordings stored in the cloud
