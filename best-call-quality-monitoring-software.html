1. Gong

Gong.ioEnterprise

Price: $100 to $200 per seat per month, annual contract

Gong is the market leader in conversation intelligence for enterprise sales teams. It records, transcribes, and analyses sales calls and demos, then surfaces insights for deal forecasting and rep coaching. Deeply integrated with Salesforce, HubSpot, and major CRMs.

Pros
Best-in-class deal intelligence
Excellent English transcription
Strong CRM integrations
Pipeline forecasting features
Cons
$100+ per seat — expensive for India
No meaningful Hindi support
Annual contract required
Not built for tele-sales or BPO

2. Chorus.ai (ZoomInfo Sales)

Chorus / ZoomInfo SalesEnterprise

Price: $80 to $140 per seat per month, annual contract

Chorus was acquired by ZoomInfo in 2021 and rebranded as ZoomInfo Sales. It provides call recording, transcription, and coaching features for B2B sales teams. Pricing has increased significantly post-acquisition.

Pros
Good meeting transcription
Solid coaching workflows
ZoomInfo data integration
Cons
No Hindi/Indic language support
Post-acquisition pricing is steep
Focused on B2B SaaS, not tele-sales

3. Observe.AI

Observe.AIEnterprise

Price: Custom enterprise pricing, 50+ seat minimum

Observe.AI is purpose-built for contact centres with real-time agent assist, automated QA scoring, and deep workforce management integrations. Powerful for large operations but inaccessible for smaller teams.

Pros
Real-time agent guidance
Automated QA at scale
Strong contact centre integrations
Cons
50+ seat minimum
Long implementation cycles
Enterprise contract required

4. Convin

Convin.aiMid-Market

Price: Custom pricing, typically Rs 800 to 1,500 per seat per month

Convin is an Indian company focused on conversation intelligence for sales and support teams. Good Hindi support, CRM integrations, and a coaching module. Better suited for mid-to-large Indian teams.

Pros
India-focused product
Reasonable Hindi support
Local support team
Cons
Not free or affordable for small teams
Minimum seat commitments
Requires onboarding

5. Enthu.AI

Enthu.AISMB

Price: $29 to $79 per seat per month

Enthu.AI targets small and mid-sized contact centres with automated quality monitoring and coaching suggestions. USD pricing makes it expensive for Indian teams. Partial Hindi support.

Pros
Lower price than Gong/Observe
Good QA automation
Easy setup
Cons
USD pricing hurts ROI for India
Incomplete Indic language support
Still requires a paid subscription

6. Clari

ClariEnterprise RevOps

Price: Enterprise only, custom pricing

Clari is a revenue operations platform with call intelligence as one component. It is designed for large B2B enterprise sales organisations with complex deal pipelines. Not relevant for tele-sales or BPO use cases.

Pros
Excellent pipeline forecasting
Enterprise RevOps suite
Cons
Not designed for call QA
Enterprise-only, very expensive
No India language support
