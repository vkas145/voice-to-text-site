Where Notta Wins

  • Real-time transcription: Notta can record and transcribe live — during meetings, interviews, or lectures. Bolo Aur Likho only transcribes uploaded files or browser recordings.
  • Chrome extension: Record Google Meet, Zoom (web), and other browser-based meetings directly with Notta's extension.
  • Speaker identification: Notta labels speakers automatically, which is valuable for meeting minutes.
  • Built-in translation: Notta can translate transcripts into 42 languages, useful for multilingual teams.
  • AI action items: Notta extracts action items and to-dos from meeting transcripts automatically.

Where Bolo Aur Likho Wins

  • Hindi and Indian languages: Bolo Aur Likho outputs Hindi in Devanagari script and handles Hinglish natively. It supports 10 Indian languages with dedicated pages. Notta's Indian language support is generic.
  • Completely free: No 120-minute monthly cap, no signup required. Notta's free tier is very restrictive.
  • More languages: 99+ vs Notta's 58 languages.
  • Privacy: Audio is processed and immediately deleted. Notta stores recordings on their servers.
  • No account needed: Upload and transcribe without creating an account or providing an email.
