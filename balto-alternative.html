Who Should Use Balto vs Bolo Aur Likho

Choose Balto if:

  • Your calls are primarily in English and you need live, real-time agent guidance
  • You have a contact centre with 200+ agents and a dedicated QA and tech team
  • You need deep CRM integration and are willing to pay enterprise pricing
  • Your business is US or UK-based with English-first customer interactions

Choose Bolo Aur Likho if:

  • Your calls are in Hindi, Hinglish, or any Indian regional language
  • You are a growing Indian contact centre, BPO, NBFC, EdTech team, or insurance team
  • You need call quality analysis immediately, without contracts or IT integration projects
  • You want to start auditing 100% of calls without hiring a full QA team first
  • Your QA process relies on transcript review and coaching conversations, not live pop-ups
💡 Post-call transcript coaching is often more effective than real-time pop-ups for complex Hindi sales calls. Agents in the middle of a nuanced objection cannot read and respond to a coaching prompt simultaneously. Transcript-anchored coaching in a feedback session changes behaviour more durably.
